Welcome, Scholar

The Digital Papyrus Project represents decades of archaeological work to preserve and digitize ancient Egyptian texts. Our team has carefully translated and cataloged hundreds of papyrus documents, revealing the wisdom of one of humanity's greatest civilizations.

You have been granted Level 1 Clearance, which provides access to our public collection of basic historical documents.

Higher clearance levels are requrired to be able to view the restricted collection and is limited to senior researchers and archaeological team leaders.
